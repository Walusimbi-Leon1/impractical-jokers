An unofficial biography of comedy's greatest friendship. This comprehensive book explores every facet of Impractical Jokers — from the origins of The Tenderloins at Monsignor Farrell High School to the show's evolution across twelve seasons.

## 📚 What's Inside

- **15 chapters** covering the show's history, format, cast, and legacy
- **20,000+ words** of in-depth analysis and storytelling
- Deep dives into each Joker: Joe Gatto, Sal Vulcano, James "Murr" Murray, and Brian "Q" Quinn
- Analysis of the show's comedy techniques, challenges, and punishments
- The fan community, behind-the-scenes production, and the show's cultural impact
